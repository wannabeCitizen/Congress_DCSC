                    EXECUTIVE REPORTS OF COMMITTEES

  The following executive reports of nominations were submitted:

       By Ms. CANTWELL for the Committee on Small Business and 
     Entrepreneurship.
       * Maria Contreras-Sweet, of California, to be Administrator 
     of the Small Business Administration.
       By Mrs. FEINSTEIN for the Select Committee on Intelligence.
       Caroline Diane Krass, of the District of Columbia, to be 
     General Counsel of the Central Intelligence Agency.
       John P. Carlin, of New York, to be an Assistant Attorney 
     General.
       Francis Xavier Taylor, of Maryland, to be Under Secretary 
     for Intelligence and Analysis, Department of Homeland 
     Security.

  * Nomination was reported with recommendation that it be confirmed 
subject to the nominee's commitment to respond to requests to appear 
and testify before any duly constituted committee of the Senate.
  (Nominations without an asterisk were reported with the 
recommendation that they be confirmed.)
